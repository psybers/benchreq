Submission Information

BESE 2019 invites novel contributions in the form of either 4-page short papers or 2-page position papers from both researchers and practitioners. Submissions can be research papers, practice papers, position papers, demo papers, or experience reports. All submissions should describe unpublished work and must have been neither previously accepted for publication nor concurrently submitted for review in another journal, book, conference, or workshop. Submissions are peer-reviewed and accepted papers will appear in the workshop proceedings.

Papers should be submitted electronically at: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=bese19

Submissions must follow the ESEC/FSE submission policies and be no more than 2 or 4 pages (including references).
